Shaneshwara is the son of Surya and vehicle is a crow. Shani is considered a stern teacher in the horoscope, which signifies discipline, asceticism, endeavor, judgment, truthfulness, yoga, and religion. When this Graha is malefic or misplaced, the person must go through hardships and unhealthy life. This Graha Shanti Jaap is performed as per the Shastras. This Jaap helps a person to get rid of the ill effects and bring happiness to life. Shani Graha Jaap is performed by invoking Shani and chanting mantras for Shani to please the Graha and the Jaap is performed as per the Shastras and Vidhan.
