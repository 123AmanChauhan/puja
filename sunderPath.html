Sunderkand Path

From ₹ 2,100.00

Sundarkand is considered to be the Heart of the Ramayan. Sunderkand Path states how Hanumanji overcame all the difficulties to find Mata Sita successfully. It also mentions how he created havoc in Lanka by burning it down to ashes. Sunderkand path is conducted in the home for different reasons like getting rid of difficulties in life, overcome opponents or enemies, Mental and physical health problems, and listening to melodious kirtans of Lord Shri Ram. This path is conducted by the main panditji beginning with Gauri Ganesh Kalash Puja, read the path accompanied by the team of pandits playing Dholak, Manjeera, and other instruments for Kirtans and Bhajans praising Lord Shri Ram and Bhakta Hanuman.

Benefits Of Sunderkand Path

  1. It wards off evil, negative energy, and enemies.
  2. Performing this path helps to attain prosperity and wealth.
  3. It helps to achieve courage and confidence.
  4. It creates peaceful surroundings and purifies the mind and the soul.
  5. It helps to get rid of diseases.

Significance of Sunderkand Path

Sundarkand Path describes Mahaveer Hanuman’s devotion along with stories of valor, wisdom, faith, dedication, and strength of Lord Hanuman. Performing this Path in our home gives us mental and physical strength and helps in getting rid of Daridrata and illness in one’s life.
