Pitru Paksha Shradh Puja

From ₹ 2,100.00

Pitru Paksha also known as Mahalaya Paksha is a 15 lunar days period when Hindus pay homage to their ancestors. Pitru paksha 2022 is from 10th September 2022 to 25th September 2022. This Shardh is performed especially through Pinda Pradhan, Tarpan, and food offerings to Brahmins. Performing this ceremony with utmost faith and a peaceful mind gives the Karta the full benefit of this ceremony. The blessings of ancestors will always be there upon them.

Benefits Of Pitru Paksha Shradh Puja

  1. By performing this ceremony one receives the Pithrus blessing to help them achieve their dreams without any obstacles.
  2. The intensity of any Pithru dosh is also reduced.

Significance of Pitru Paksha Shradh Puja

Pitru paksha or Mahalaya paksha is a 15 lunar days period wherein the Pitrus are allowed to come to earth. The elder most son performs the shradh and other members participate in the ceremony. Brahman bhojan is given to get the blessings from pitrus.
