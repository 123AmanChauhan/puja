Significance of Laxmi Kubera Havan

The ancient story behind the origination of Lord Kuber is dated back to the time of God and goddesses. Lord Kuber was quite obese in shape and all the other lords made fun of him. Therefore, Lord Kuber did a severe penance to appease Lord Shiva who granted his wish and made him the lord of wealth. Thus, everyone started admiring and respecting him. Since then Lord Kuber is prayed together with Maa Lakshmi to bless the devotees with money and prosperity.
