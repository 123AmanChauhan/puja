Mata Ki Chowki

From ₹ 2,100.00

Mata Ki Chowki is a spiritual program that can be kept at any time or performed on any special occasions such as Sagai, birthdays, Marriages, anniversaries, or during Ganapati and Navaratri festivals. It includes Bhajans and Kirtans in the praise of Mata rani who is the divine mother. All the devotees or the family members gather together and enjoy the beautiful bhajans. This path is conducted by the main panditji beginning with Gauri Ganesh Kalash Puja, who read the path accompanied by the team of pandits playing Dholak, Manjeera, and other instruments for Kirtans and Bhajans praising the Mata Durga.
