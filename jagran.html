Jagran is performed at night with all the relatives and neighbors gathered together. They devote time in the prayers for Lord. Panditji performed a small puja with Gauri Ganesh Kalash, Navgraha Puja, and then the kirtans and bhajans are performed.Jāgran or jāgrata or jaag is a Hindu ritual, mainly prevalent in North India, consisting of all-night vigil, songs and dance in honour of a deity and puja.

Benefits Of Jagran Puja

  1. This puja purifies and spiritualizes the environment and surroundings of the home with holy vibes and a divine ambiance.
  2. It helps to overcome and remove any obstacles and hurdles of a person that come across in life.
  3. Jagran helps to increase the devotion towards the Lord.

Significance of Jagran Puja

Jagran is performed usually at night in the praise of the Lord by singing Kirtans and Bhajans and devotees engage themselves in the Kirtans by clapping and dancing.
